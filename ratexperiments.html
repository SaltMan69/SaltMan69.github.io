Rat Experiments

The little rat is alone

They are performing tests on it

Subjecting it to their own hypothesis

They call it science

But the life at stake is not their own

An objector could say that the rat experiments are cruel

And one of the scientists could even say that this was true

But they do not understand...

The rat is a social creature

The experiments may be cruel

But the worst part is that the rat is alone

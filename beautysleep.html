Beauty Sleep

She never slept

So she became quite hideous

A young man in passing told her

"Get your beauty sleep."

But she did not like the way he said it

Like a command

Maybe if he had phrased it as a question...

"Perhaps, you should get your beauty sleep?"

Maybe then she would've slept

But what if she didn't mind being hideous?

Maybe hideousity was her goal?

Maybe instead of sleeping and being beautiful

She wanted to be alive and hideous
